# WELCOME TO CLIMATEDATA.CA's INTERACTIVE CONTAMINATED SITES CLIMATE DECISION SUPPORT TOOL

## This interactive tool is designed for contaminated sites custodians and other professionals in the contaminated sites sector.  The tool has two main goals:

- **GOAL 1** - Gain a better sense of how to select useful climate change information for contaminated sites climate change considerations, including risk assessments and long term planning.

- **GOAL 2** - Help find some  useful climate change information for your contaminated site

## You will be guided through the following 5 Steps:

- **STEP 1** - Provide basic information about your contaminated site
- **STEP 2** - Develop an inventory of contaminated site components and systems
- **STEP 3** - Identify the local weather and climate hazards that may matter for your contaminated site
- **STEP 4** - Explore the impacts of weather and climate hazards on contaminated site components and systems
- **STEP 5** - Summary report and next steps

## This process should take around 20 minutes to complete!  Have fun!
